Revamp on Tarren Mill EU is a recently transferred semi-hardcore guild with ambitions to break into the world top 1000 guild rankings.

We are a group of experienced mature players that try to achieve rapid progression while raiding only two days per week. To accomplish our goals, we have a strict approach to raiding. Our process begins with comprehensive preparation for each fight.

During progression encounters we prioritise mechanics over racing in the meters. After raids we analyse logs and help our raiders become the best they can be. Read more about our rules here.
